Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Internet Included?

A: No, the internet is not included. You need to provide your own internet or an existing one will work.

Q: Do you offer installment?

A: Not at this moment maybe soon. For now, we are only accepting cash and one time payment.

Q: Do you sell the machine outside Bacolod City?

A: We do but we recommend that you find the nearest distributor when acquiring to have a reachable technical support.

Q: What's included in the package?

A: It comes with 2 meters internet cable and a free banner.

Q: Do I need pay to a franchise or recurring fee?

A: No need, the fee is only one time with 3 months of warranty.

Q: Can I view my sales remotely?

A: Yes, you can. You will be given an access upon request.

Q: Do machine accept piso coin only?

A: The machine accepts 1,5 and 10 old and new piso coins.

Q: How do I access the admin dashboard?

A: Connect to the NegrosPisoWifi then go to any browser and access 10.0.0.1/admin and type in credentials that we gave you.

Q: Is time adjustable?

A: Yes, it is. The default timer setting is 1=6mins, 5=30mins and 10=1hr. Go to admin then timer settings to make changes.

Q: What ISP do you recommend?

A: It depends in your area. Always check with the providers, the machine will work on either wired or not.

Q: How do I generate the voucher codes?

A: You get these codes from admin dashboard. Go to /admin then go to Voucher then generate base on the days/hours you need.

Q: Is it Cash On Delivery?

A: Yes, definitely.
