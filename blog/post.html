Piso Wi-Fi vending machine, a new booming business

Posted by Dale on March 15, 2017

Vending machines can dispense chips, chocolates, sodas, water, coffee, and many more. We even had charging kiosk at malls and convenient stores plus piso-internet machines for cheap PC rental. This time around, why don’t we aim higher.

I’m talking about this Wi-Fi vending machine we saw online. This Wi-Fi enabled machine can give you instant Wi-Fi hotspot for as low as Php 1. It uses an IEE802.11 b/g/n compatible router at 2.4Ghz band.

The process is simple.

  • You connect to NegrosPisoWifi.
  • Tap insert a coin to the machine and it will give you 30 seconds
  • drop coins then start internet!

    • Wi-Fi rates are as follows:

  • Php 1 = 5 minutes
  • Php 5 = 25 minutes
  • Php 10 = 50 minutes

    • When you have existing internet that is costing you monthly with no returns, why not earn from it? This machine only costs around 18k-20kphp depending on the design.

    Giving you some breakdown of possible returns:

  • 20 customers x 1php / hour = 20php x 8 hrs = 160php x 30 days = 4800php
  • 20 customers x 5php / hour = 100php x 8 hrs = 800php x 30 days = 21000php
  • 20 customers x 10php / hour = 200php x 8 hrs = 1600php x 30 days = 48000php

    • I hope my math is correct. :)

    The machine is very easy to install and manage. It's paperless and you can view the sales history remotely anywhere in the world.
